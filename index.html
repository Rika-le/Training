Our Story

Founded in 2010 by renowned chef Michael Blackwood, Savoria began as a small bistro with a vision to create an exceptional dining experience that celebrates the art of gastronomy.

Over the years, we've grown into one of the city's most beloved culinary destinations, known for our commitment to quality ingredients, innovative techniques, and warm hospitality.

Our philosophy is simple: we believe that dining should be an experience that engages all the senses and creates lasting memories. Every dish tells a story, every ingredient has a purpose, and every guest is treated like family.

Meet Our Chef

Executive Chef Michael Blackwood brings over 20 years of culinary expertise to Savoria. After training in Paris and working in Michelin-starred restaurants across Europe, he returned to his hometown to create a dining experience that combines classical techniques with contemporary innovation.

Chef Blackwood's philosophy centers on respecting ingredients, supporting local producers, and creating dishes that surprise and delight without unnecessary complexity.

"My goal is to create food that speaks to both the heart and the palate. Every dish should tell a story and create a memory." - Chef Michael Blackwood
